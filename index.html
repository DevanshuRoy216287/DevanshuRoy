Devanshu Roy

I've always been curious about how things work. I like learning by actually doing things—whether it’s figuring stuff out on my own, trying new ideas, or solving problems. I enjoy challenges that make me think in new ways and get better at what I do. The more I explore, the more I realize how much there is to learn, and that just makes me want to keep going.

"I love coding and designing websites, whether it's crafting layouts or working behind the scenes. I focus on clean, responsive designs and experiment with JavaScript, python, and tools like bootstrap to make sites more interactive. It tinkles my creative side."

"Building robots is a passion of mine. I spend time fine-tuning my robot soccer bots for better movement and response. Using ESP32, Arduino, and sensors, I work on motor control and strategies to improve their performance in matches."

"I love using Blender to create 3D models. Whether it's designing something or experimenting, I enjoy seeing an idea take shape. I mostly create robot parts, mechanical structures, and custom designs for different projects. I also try out rendering and animation to make my models look more realistic."
